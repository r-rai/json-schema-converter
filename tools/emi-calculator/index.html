About This Tool

Calculate equated monthly installments for loans. View detailed amortization schedule and interest breakdown.

Privacy First Client-Side Only Open Source

help How to Use This Tool

Quick Start

  1. Loan Amount: Enter total loan amount (e.g., ₹5,00,000)
  2. Interest Rate: Enter annual interest rate as percentage (e.g., 10 for 10%)
  3. Loan Tenure: Enter loan duration in years (e.g., 5)
  4. Calculate: Click "Calculate EMI" to see monthly payment and breakdown

Understanding Results

  • Monthly EMI: Fixed amount to pay each month
  • Total Amount: Sum of all EMI payments over loan tenure
  • Total Interest: Extra amount paid beyond principal
  • Amortization Table: Month-by-month payment breakdown

Tips

  • Shorter tenure = higher EMI but less total interest
  • Longer tenure = lower EMI but more total interest
  • Chart shows principal vs interest proportion over time
  • Scroll amortization table to see detailed monthly breakdown

Prepayment Calculator

  1. Calculate EMI First: Main EMI must be calculated before prepayment
  2. Enter Prepayment Details: Amount, month, and option (reduce tenure or EMI)
  3. See Savings: View interest saved, time saved, and updated loan details

💡 Tip: Prepayments in early years save more interest due to higher interest component in initial EMIs.
