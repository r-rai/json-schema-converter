help How to Use This Tool

Quick Start

  1. Monthly Investment: Enter amount you'll invest each month (e.g., ₹5,000)
  2. Expected Return: Enter annual return rate as percentage (e.g., 12 for 12%)
  3. Time Period: Enter investment duration in years (e.g., 10)
  4. Calculate: Click "Calculate SIP" to see projections and growth chart

Understanding Results

  • Total Investment: Sum of all monthly contributions (Principal)
  • Interest Earned: Compound interest accumulated over time
  • Total Value: Final portfolio value (Investment + Interest)

Tips

  • Historical equity mutual fund returns: 10-15% annually
  • Longer time periods dramatically increase returns due to compounding
  • Chart shows growth trajectory over investment period
